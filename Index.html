About Optiks

Think Different. Think Big. Think Fast.

Founded by Mr. Kapil Kaushik, a visionary with over 25 years of experience in the lighting and tools industry, Optiks Mechatronics pioneered in-house LED lens development. We are driving the "Made in India" movement with precision optics.

Mission: Deliver high precision optical lenses and lighting products that improve efficiency, sustainability, and innovation.

Objective: Create lighting solutions that enhance architectural spaces with precision optics and superior performance.
